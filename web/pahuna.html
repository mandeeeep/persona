Pahuna

Pahuna, it’s more of a room reservation system, that covers some basic hotel based functionalities like billing requisites, in-house restaurant requisites and walk-in registrations. The room bookings are online based. The web application is being developed for a medium scale hotel business Hotel Lakestar, Pokhara. The modules and the system was designed and planned as according to requirement of the client. It’s still in its development phase.
